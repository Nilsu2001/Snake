How to play Snake

Use WASD or the arrow keys to control the snake.

Collect cookies to score points.

Each collected cookie increases the speed and makes the snake grow.

If a snowball is collected, the speed decreases and the snake will shrink.

Be careful! The snowballs will change their position.

Moving the snake through the tunnel allows it to cross itself.

The game is over when the snake collides with itself or the edge of the playing area.

The snake will also die as soon as it is less than 3 parts long.
